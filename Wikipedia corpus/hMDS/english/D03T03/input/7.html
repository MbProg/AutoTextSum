This bill was an early attempt to establish a mental health care system in Alaska when it was still a US Territory ( Alaska became a State on January 3, 1959). The intent of the bill was to use forestry and mining monies derived from US-owned land in Alaska to self-fund a mental health care system in Alaska. Read the Wiki article and you will see that fringe groups got involved. They feared the polio vaccine, fluoridated water, and psychiatry. The following line spoken in 1956 sure sounds familiar:

Mrs. Ernest W. Howard of the Women's Patriotic Committee on National Defense castigated the slackness of Congress for not picking up on the bill's perceived dangers: "Those of us who have been in the study and research work of the United Nations, we feel that we are experts in this . . .

ref: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alaska_Men ... abling_Act

"We are the experts. We are the authorities. We don't need permission...." These are the lines spoken by Cultists of the world who want to tell the rest of us what is best for us -- and what is dangerous to their pocketbooks, bigotries, and poisonous ideologies:

John Kaspar, a White Citizens' Council organizer who had achieved notoriety for starting a race riot in Clinton, Tennessee, declared that "almost one hundred percent of all psychiatric therapy is Jewish and about eighty percent of psychiatrists are Jewish . . . one particular race is administering this particular thing." He argued that Jews were nationalists of another country who were attempting to "usurp American nationality."

ref: ibid.
