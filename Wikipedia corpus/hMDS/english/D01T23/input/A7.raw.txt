Buckingham Palace is the official London residence of Queen Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom and her consort Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh. Buckingham Palace is a setting for state occasions and royal entertaining and a major tourist attraction.
The palace's forecourt including the Victoria Memorial has been a rallying point for the British people at times of national rejoicing and crisis.
