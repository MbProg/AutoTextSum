“"The Bean" ”
Reviewed 2 weeks ago via mobile

Not sure why it's called "The Bean" except that it looks like a giant bean. It's pretty crowed on the weekends, but u can find a time when there are not that many people around if you like to get those pictures with less people in them, u just still might have to wait a little for that perfect moment, it's in a pretty popular spot and always seems to have a few people around it. But it's a pretty neat piece of art and something worth checking out, plus it's free and something you don't see every day.
