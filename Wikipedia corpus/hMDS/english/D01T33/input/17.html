Anish Kapoor’s Cloud Gate

Millennium Park, Chicago, Illinois, 2005

Site Project Supervisor, MTH Industries & Performance Structures

Seamlessly welded piece, crafted from individual sheets of stainless steel, which were hand-rolled and polished. To ensure that each sheet was an exact color match, the steel came from a single ingot, poured in Sweden.

Materials:
Stainless Steel

Dimensions:
33 feet high x
66 feet wide x
42 feet long
