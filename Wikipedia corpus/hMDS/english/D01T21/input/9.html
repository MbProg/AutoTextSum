Icy Nuffield College

Begun in 1949, the buildings of Nuffield College by Austin Harrison have a Lutyen-esque feel. Based on "Cotswolds domestic architecture", its sense of symmetry is enhanced by the formal reflecting pool and long garden, seen here on a cold winter day. Source: Lawrence OP on Flickr
