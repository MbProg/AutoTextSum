Torment and graphite.
Posted on
April 18, 2013
by labellerire
Standard
A graphite drawing, female nude model. Based on Amoret, the figure in Etty’s ‘Britomart Redeems Faire Amoret’. In this the woman warrior Britomart rescues Amoret from the cruel hands of the enchanter and torturer, Busyrane. The title seemed fitting to this scene.
