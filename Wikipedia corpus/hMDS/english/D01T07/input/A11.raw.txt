Battle of Issus
(Albrecht Altdorfer's (circa 1480 -
1538) most famous painting, The     Battle of Alexander at Issus, known by various similar titles such as Battle of Alexander,     Battle of Issus, etc., depicts the Battle of Issus, and Alexander the Great's victory     over Darius III of Persia in 333 BC.)
(1529, oil on panel, 158 × 120 cm (62.2 × 47.2     in), currently @ Alte Pinakothek, München.)
