Bramall Hall 
The iconic Bramall Hall is a Tudor manor house in Bramhall.  The manor of Bramall was first described in the Domesday Book in 1086.

Both a tourist attraction and popular with local families, the property is set in 60 acres of parkland with lakes, woods and gardens.
