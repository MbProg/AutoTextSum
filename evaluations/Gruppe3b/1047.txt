Summary of special education needs of gifted children.

Gifted children are considered special needs children.
Although their learning comprehension may exceed their age, the emotional needs of the child may not.
Parents of gifted children typically have few opportunities to talk with other parents of gifted children.
Students who are both gifted and learning disabled do not always receive service for both areas.
ACT believes that children should be encouraged to reach their maximum potential.
Schools are there to support the education that parents seek for their children.
Gifted children have been short-changed by the Labour Government.
